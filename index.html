Lollipops!

By Lucas Lopez and Vandre Nobrega

Plain lollipops are made up of very few but vital ingredients. Primarily a lollipop contains sugar, corn syrup, water, food additives and flavourings and malic or citric acid. But ultimately the important ingredients are sugar and corn syrup.
